UIPath

Website Automation using UIPath

This project was professional in nature. A fellow coworker and I were tasked with transfering the Video and Caption Data from One Media Hosting service to another.

Due to this project being a one time operation with a timeline, we designed our automation system to be minimal and adaptable, as the primary reason for automating this process in the first place was saving time.

After determining the number of steps required to fully complete a transfer by completing several manually, my coworker and I determined that automation was necessary as each transfer required close to a hundred steps between all of the various stages of transfer, and with eigthy-seven hundred transfers needing completion manual transfering was not feasible with just the two of us dedicated to this project.

Due to the nature of the multiple stages of the transfer process my coworker and I opted to not create a single automation process but instead break it up into several independent programs to make troubleshooting easier as the goal was to minimize time as much as possible.
